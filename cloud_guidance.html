Cloud guidance

What are the 5 pillars of the AWS and Azure Well-Architected Framework?

The 5 pillars of the AWS and Microsoft Azure Well-Architected Framework helps cloud users build secure, high-performing, resilient, and efficient infrastructure for their applications.

The 5 Pillars:

  1. Operational Excellence
  2. Security
  3. Reliability
  4. Performance Efficiency
  5. Reliability

How does the 5 pillars fit in to the Home Office guidance?

We have adapted the 5 pillars of the Well Architected Framework from our cloud providers (AWS and Azure) to organise and create new guidance for the Home Office. The guidance from the cloud providers is useful as a high-level overview and we have provided the links to this in the further reading below. However, we wanted to create guidance pages that are specific to cloud in the Home Office and we have placed guidance pages under the appropriate pillars. We have also adapted Home Office technical architecture and security principles into the guidance.

Further resources

It is still important to make use of service specific and general guidance provided by AWS and Azure. The guidance we have created does not cover all aspects of the framework or the use of services on AWS or Azure. Our guidance is a starting point and is to help you with Home Office specific queries.

Well-Architected Frameworks:

  • The AWS Well Architected Framework will give you an overview of the five pillars. It also gives you access to the whitepapers that goes into great depth for each of the pillars.
  • The Microsoft Azure Well Architected Framework will give you an overview of the five pillars. It also gives you access to the whitepapers that goes into great depth for each of the pillars.
