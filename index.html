Home Office - Cloud Centre of Excellence (HO-CCoE)

This service has been created by the Public Cloud Team (PCT) and it is replacing the Public Cloud confluence area. The HO-CCoE has been designed to support Home Office employees that use or have an interest in the cloud.

Service overview

The main sections of the service include:

Cloud training

This section of the service shows the cloud training available and how to book training. It also has a breakdown of Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure’s training and certification pathways.

Cloud guidance

This section of the service has Home Office cloud related guidance. We divided the guidance in to ‘The Five Pillars of the Well-Architected Framework’ from AWS and Azure best practice guidance.

Cloud services

This section of the service shows the different services that the Public Cloud Team offers. This includes AWS IAM which shows you how to get started in AWS and how to modify your existing account cloud account.

Getting in touch

The Public Cloud Team are an agile team based in Manchester and Croydon. Our roles in the Home Office include:

  • Governing the use of cloud across the department.
  • Enabling teams to access the cloud and ensure that cloud accounts are secured and monitored.
  • Provide support and guidance to make better use of public cloud services from our cloud providers, AWS & Azure.

This is a live service, and we will be updating and improving the service regularly. For any other queries or support you need that hasn’t been included on this service please contact: public.cloud@homeoffice.gov.uk
